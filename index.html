dRonin

An autopilot/flight controller firmware for controllers in the OpenPilot/Tau Labs family. It's aimed at a variety of use cases: acro/racing, autonomous flight, and vehicle research.

Setup faster

With dRonin's easy setup wizard and advanced Autotuning it has never been faster to fly a well tuned machine. For details on getting started please see our documentation and Wiki.

Open source

dRonin is production ready and released under the GPL.

Hardware

dRonin supports several flight control boards including: BrainFPV, Sparky 1, Sparky 2, Revo, CC3D, AeroQuad32, Colibri, Quantec Quanton, Lumenier LUX and Naze32.
